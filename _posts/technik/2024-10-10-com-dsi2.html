MIPI DSI-2


Example

LT9611UXC

  • Receiver: MIPI CSI/DSI
  • Transmitter: HDMI 2.0
  • I2C address: 0x5E (when I2C_ADDR is high), 0x56 (when I2C_ADDR is low)
    • i2c_write(0xff, 0x80) //switch to bank address 0x80
i2c_write(0xee, 0x01) //write 0x01 to 0x80ee (Stop firmware in OCM)
i2c_write(0xff, 0x81) //switch to bank address 0x81
chip_id_0 = i2c_read(0x00) //read chip ID
chip_id_1 = i2c_read(0x01)
chip_id_2 = i2c_read(0x02)
  • Debug: UART Tx (GPIO4), Baudrate 62500
